Join the Treehouse team and help us achieve our vision of diversifying the tech industry through accessible education and apprenticeship!

Our Mission

We are committed to Equity, Diversity & Inclusion at every touch point and understand the value that a diverse team gives to our business and community. Our pursuit of an equitable working environment seeks to address systemic disparities so that each student and employee can participate in enjoying a healthy, rewarding, and supportive work environment. Our diverse backgrounds make us better, stronger and more innovative.

Our headquarters are located in Portland, OR but many members of our team work remotely.

Our Perks

Take care of your work and Treehouse takes good care of you. We believe in delight for your loved ones as well as the Treehouse mission.

Work-life integration

Time with loved ones is important. We offer flexible schedules, 12 weeks paid maternity leave, and 4 weeks paid paternity leave.

Full health coverage

Medical, dental, and vision for you and your family. Coverage begins on day one.

401(k) matching

Invest in your future with 100% matching for up to 6% of your salary. Matching begins on day one.

Ample time off

3+ weeks PTO, federal holidays, and 1 week off in late December.

Education budget

Budget each year for conferences or to learn anything you want.

Wellness allowance

Budget each year to invest in what you need to stay healthy and happy.
